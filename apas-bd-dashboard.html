Section 07

Operations, Quality & Risk Governance

Consistent delivery, plus the policy gaps a defence-adjacent firm can't skip.

Delivery lifecycle

  • Discovery & scoping (1–3 days) — confirm the question, not just the topic
  • Research & primary-source gathering (40–60% of timeline)
  • Internal peer review — every report, second eyes, before delivery
  • Client delivery & walkthrough call
  • 14-day follow-up — satisfaction check + retainer pitch

Conflict of interest & liability policy (new)

Conflict of interest

No engagements assessing Indian armed forces operational posture, procurement the founder was personally involved in, or competing vendors in an active bid. Standing check at every discovery call.

Forecasting liability

Every report carries a standard disclaimer — forecasts are probabilistic, liability capped at engagement fee. Contracts reviewed by external counsel before first signature.
